Step by Step

How to Apply

Choose your track below — the process is streamlined either way, and most candidates complete registration and testing within a week.

  1. 01
    Submit Enquiry Form

    Fill the online enquiry form or visit our Susan Road campus front desk with basic student details.

  2. 02
    Document Verification

    Submit required documents for verification and receive your provisional registration slip.

  3. 03
    Entry Assessment

    Sit a short placement test in core subjects — used for streaming and mentor allocation, not rejection.

  4. 04
    Fee Submission & Confirmation

    Pay the admission fee at our accounts office or via bank transfer to confirm your seat.

  2. 02
    Document & Medical Screening

    Submit documents plus a basic fitness/medical self-declaration for mentor allocation.

  3. 03
    Diagnostic Test

    Sit a diagnostic covering academic and intelligence-test style questions to plan your prep schedule.

  4. 04
    Fee Submission & Batch Assignment

    Confirm your seat and get assigned to a dedicated entry-test preparation batch.
